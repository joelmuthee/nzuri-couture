Nzuri Couture is a Nairobi boutique for women who dress with intention. We curate dresses, two-piece sets, jumpsuits, skirts, outerwear, shoes and accessories, with fresh styles landing every week.

How to buy

  1. Tap Enquire on the item you want. It opens WhatsApp with the item pre-filled.
  2. Confirm availability and size, then pay via M-Pesa (we share the details on WhatsApp).
  3. Pick up in Kilimani, Nairobi, or we can arrange delivery anywhere in Kenya.
